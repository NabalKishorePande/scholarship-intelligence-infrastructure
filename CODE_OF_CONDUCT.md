# Code of Conduct

The Scholarship Intelligence Infrastructure™ exists to improve decision quality through respectful collaboration, evidence-based discussion, and constructive feedback.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful.
- Be professional.
- Focus on ideas rather than individuals.
- Support claims with evidence whenever possible.
- Help improve the quality of the infrastructure.

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

- Personal attacks
- Harassment
- Spam
- Hate speech
- Plagiarism
- Deliberate misinformation

The maintainer reserves the right to moderate discussions to preserve the integrity of the repository.

Thank you for contributing responsibly.
