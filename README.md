# Scholarship Intelligence Infrastructure™

> **Technical Infrastructure Repository**

A structured, evidence-based initiative for building decision systems, research infrastructure, and knowledge assets that improve how students discover, evaluate, prepare for, and execute fully funded Master's opportunities.

---

# Vision

The Scholarship Intelligence Infrastructure™ is not a collection of documents.

It is a continuously evolving knowledge infrastructure designed to transform research into practical decision intelligence through interconnected frameworks, datasets, technical systems, and execution workflows.

Every asset increases the long-term **Knowledge Capital** of the infrastructure.

---

# Purpose

This repository serves as the technical foundation of the Scholarship Intelligence Infrastructure™.

Rather than publishing disconnected resources, the infrastructure emphasizes:

- Evidence before opinion
- Systems before content
- Architecture before implementation
- Long-term stewardship
- Continuous improvement

---

# Engineering Principles

This repository follows five engineering principles.

## Knowledge Capital

Nothing should exist in isolation.

---

## Functional Separation

---

## Version Everything

---

## Build for AI and Humans

Knowledge should remain understandable by both people and intelligent systems.

## Institutional Continuity

The infrastructure remains coherent regardless of future platform, technology, or implementation changes.
