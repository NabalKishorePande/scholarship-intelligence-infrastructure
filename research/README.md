# Research

Research is the foundation of the Scholarship Intelligence Infrastructure™.

Every framework, dataset, workflow, article, and decision system originates from structured research.

This directory documents research projects from initial questions through evidence collection, analysis, publication, and long-term maintenance.

---

# Research Philosophy

Research should be:

- Evidence-based
- Transparent
- Versioned
- Reproducible whenever possible
- Structured for both humans and AI systems
